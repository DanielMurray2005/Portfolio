Functional 2-Player Chess Game

I created this project as a means of exploring recursion. I had recently learned about recursion in my Computer Science class in 11th grade, so I used it to gather lists of legal moves for various pieces. I used a 2-dimensional array to represent the chess board. This project still lacks special moves like castling and en passant, however, it works fairly well. Ultimately, recursion is not the most efficient way to gather a piece's legal moves, but it was an enjoyable exploration.
